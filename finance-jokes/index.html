Best finance jokes and financial jokes about money

These finance jokes lean into investing, debt, banking, present value, risk, cash flow, and capital. If you searched for finance jokes or financial jokes, this is the tighter page built for that exact intent.

  1. Why can't the car payment make any friends?
    Because they're always "a loan"
  2. What did the financially responsible student do to get good grades?
    They paid off their "principal"
  3. Why did the financial analyst give his daughter gifts today instead of waiting until Christmas?
    Because he understands "Present Value"
  4. Why do investors always buy Altoids?
    They're great invest-mints!
  5. Why did the cowboy walk into the financial advisor's office?
    He was told it was a bull market
  6. Why did Grizzly Adams walk into the financial advisor's office?
    He was told it was a bear market
  7. Why should you buy stock in the boulder company?
    Because it's a rock solid investment!
  8. What kind of debt did the secret agent issue?
    A bond, James Bond.
  9. Why was the banker bad at playing music?
    Because they only knew how to play a Treasury note
  10. I bite all my quarters...
    Now I have a lot of bitcoin
  11. Why did the investor think he could sell his lakefront property quickly?
    Because it was liquid
  12. I stopped inviting Diversification over for board game night...
    They never wanted to play "Risk"
  13. Did you hear about the creditor who got bored?
    He lost interest
  14. Why did the clown business go bankrupt after 5 years?
    They had a large balloon loan
  15. Did you hear about the frog who had $50,000 in debt?
    He "toad" a lot of money
  16. I had to take out a loan for my lawn equipment...
    It was a great Annual Percentage Rake (APR)
  17. I just invested in a bakery...
    It was supposed to have a great RO-Pie
  18. What does a volcano go into when it can't pay its mortgage?
    Bankruption
  19. What type of credit line does a door company have?
    Revolving
  20. What's it called when a Mastercard kicks a field goal?
    Credit score!
  21. Why couldn't they sell the Tower of Pisa?
    There was a "lien" on it
  22. Why did the businessman throw all his money in the river?
    He wanted to see his cash flow
  23. Did you hear about the well-funded alphabet company?
    They had 26 sources of "capital"
  24. Why was the fishing store so valuable?
    It had a lot of "net" worth
