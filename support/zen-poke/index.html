Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I download Zen Poke?

Zen Poke is available on the App Store. For current platform links, use the main Zen Poke page.

Is Zen Poke on Android?

Use the Android status page for the current public update.

What kind of game is Zen Poke?

Zen Poke is a calm, satisfying felt-style puzzle game with short sessions, portrait-first controls, and offline-friendly core play.

Can I send gameplay or feel feedback?

Yes. Feedback about pacing, comfort, clarity, and how the game feels in your hands is especially useful.

How should I report a crash or bug?

Please include device model, OS version, app version, and the steps you took right before the issue. That is usually enough to reproduce and fix most problems faster.
