Frequently Asked Questions

The app is still in development. Can I download it?

Not yet. Public store links will be added to the Zen Poke app page once release builds are available.

What kind of experience is Zen Poke aiming for?

Zen Poke is being built as a calm, relaxing puzzle experience with tactile-feeling interactions and short sessions that help you reset instead of getting stuck in endless scrolling.

Can I send gameplay or feel feedback?

Yes. Feedback about pacing, comfort, clarity, and how the game feels in-hand is especially helpful while the project is still in development.

How should I report a crash or bug?

Please include device model, OS version, app version, and the steps you took right before the issue. That is usually enough to reproduce and fix most problems faster.
