Zen Poke FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Zen Poke

This page answers the most common questions about Zen Poke, including what kind of game it is, where to download it, and where to find support.

What is Zen Poke?

Zen Poke is a calm felt-style puzzle game built for short, satisfying sessions. The core loop is based on gradually revealing soft art scenes one touch at a time.

What kind of players is Zen Poke for?

It is a strong fit for people who want a cozy, low-pressure mobile game that feels relaxing rather than intense. It works especially well for short breaks and evening wind-down time.

Where can I download Zen Poke?

You can download Zen Poke on the App Store. For the latest public availability summary, use the main Zen Poke page.

Is Zen Poke on Android?

The Android rollout has its own public update page at Zen Poke Android. That page will be updated once Google Play availability is confirmed.

Can I play Zen Poke offline?

Zen Poke has offline-friendly core gameplay, which makes it useful for short sessions, travel, and situations where you do not want to depend on a constant connection.

Does Zen Poke have daily puzzles?

Yes. Daily puzzle play is part of the rhythm of the game, alongside progression, stars, and streaks.

Where can I get support for Zen Poke?

Use the Zen Poke support page or email wordena16@gmail.com.

Where can I learn how the game works?

Start with How to Play Zen Poke for a quick explanation of the reveal loop and progression flow.
