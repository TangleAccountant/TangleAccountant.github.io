Zen Poke

Calm Satisfying Felt Reveal. Poke fluffy felt art and watch cozy handmade scenes come to life.

Free on the App Store iPhone + iPad Offline-first core loop

Zen Poke is a calm, satisfying felt reveal game built for portrait play. Slowly uncover finished handmade scenes, one soft reveal at a time. Relax with daily reveals, offline-friendly play, stars, streaks, and cosmetic unlocks. From animals and flowers to food, fantasy, travel, and nature scenes, each puzzle is designed for players seeking a soothing game they can pick up anytime and enjoy at their own pace.

Calm-First Design Philosophy

Zen Poke is designed as a cozy, tactile puzzle experience that helps you slow down, focus, and settle into a calmer headspace.

It is built as a healthier alternative to compulsive scrolling: something you can open for a few minutes, feel grounded, and leave in a better state of mind.
