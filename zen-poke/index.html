Zen Poke

Zen Poke is a relaxing and calming iPhone game built for short, low-pressure mobile sessions and soft poke-to-reveal progress.

Free on the App Store iPhone + iPad 100 puzzles live now Offline-friendly core play Android page available

If you want a relaxing game for iPhone that feels soothing instead of noisy, Zen Poke is built for that exact moment. You poke through soft felt-style artwork, watch scenes come together one touch at a time, and play daily goals, progression levels, and replayable sessions without needing a lot of mental energy.

100 relaxing puzzles are available right now, with new ones added regularly.

The core appeal is simple: a satisfying reveal mechanic, controls that feel natural on your phone, and calm pacing that works well during short breaks, evening wind-down time, or whenever you want something gentle to do on your phone.
