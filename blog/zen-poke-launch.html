Zen Poke Is Live on the App Store

Published on March 25, 2026

Zen Poke is live. You can download it right now, for free, on the App Store.

For the current public hub, use the main Zen Poke page. You can also jump directly to the FAQ, Android status page, or support page.

What Zen Poke Is

Zen Poke is a calm felt-style puzzle game built for portrait play. You poke through soft art layers and watch cozy scenes come together one reveal at a time.

It is designed for players who want something satisfying and easy to settle into. The pacing is intentionally gentle, the sessions can be short, and the experience works well when you want a break from noisier, more demanding games.

What Is In The Game

  • Satisfying fluffy-to-finished felt reveals
  • Calm touch-first portrait gameplay
  • Daily puzzle sessions, stars, streaks, and level progression
  • Replay and share after completion
  • Cozy cosmetic unlocks as you play
  • Offline-friendly core gameplay

The game is free with an optional one-time purchase to remove ads.

Download and Learn More

Zen Poke is available on the App Store for iPhone and iPad. Android status updates live on the site and will be updated once Google Play approval is complete.
