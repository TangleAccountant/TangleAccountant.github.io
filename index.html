Zen Poke

Zen Poke is a relaxing and calming mobile game designed to feel comfortable on your phone. You slowly reveal soft art scenes, make visible progress with every tap, and play in short, low-pressure sessions that feel more soothing than demanding.

100 relaxing puzzles are available right now, with new ones added regularly.

The game is designed for people who want something cozy and tactile-feeling to do when they do not want a loud, high-stress mobile game. It works well for short breaks, evening wind-down time, and casual daily play, with a poke-to-reveal loop that makes every tap feel like progress.
