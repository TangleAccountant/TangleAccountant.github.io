About

My friends call me worden and I used to work in an accounting department at a large company.

This site exists because I have an unhealthy obsession with finance. My favorite definition of finance has always been "the art and science of managing money. Art AND Science.

There's a lot to unpack there. But for now all you need to know is that I started my career interested in corporate finance. That evolved into personal finance. And once that was tapped out I moved onto decentralized finance. Each of these coexist with each other in such a neat way. Hopefully I can look back on these pages when I'm older and see where everything went right or where it all went wrong.
